Eight services, one number to call

Whatever stage your water system is at — first fit, repair, or full overhaul — Dream Flush carries the trade and the parts to see it through.

01

Leak Detection & Pipe Repair

Concealed & exposed lines

We trace hidden leaks behind walls, under slabs and along risers using pressure testing and moisture tracking, then repair or re-route the affected section with matched fittings — so you fix the actual break, not just the wet patch.

02

Borehole Drilling & Water Treatment

Pump sizing included

Site survey, borehole drilling, casing and pump installation, paired with filtration and treatment so what comes out of the tap is clean and pressure-consistent, even during dry-season peaks.

03

Drainage & Sewage Systems

Soakaway & septic design

Blocked-drain clearing, soakaway pit construction and septic tank installation planned around your plot's drainage and soil conditions, to keep waste water moving away from the building.

04

Bathroom & Kitchen Fit-Out

Sanitary ware supply

Full plumbing for new bathrooms and kitchens — WCs, basins, showers, sinks and taps supplied and fitted, with waterproofing and pipe routing planned before tiling starts.

05

Water Heater & Pump Installation

Sized to household demand

Instant and storage water heaters, plus booster and surface pumps, selected for your tank height, household size and pressure needs, and wired safely into the water line.

06

24/7 Emergency Plumbing

Dispatch within the hour

Burst pipes, overflowing tanks and failed pumps don't wait for business hours — neither do we. Call the line above any time and a technician is on the way.

07

Estate & Facility Maintenance Contracts

Scheduled inspection

Recurring maintenance plans for estates, schools and offices — scheduled inspections, priority callout response and a standing relationship with one contractor for every fitting.

08

Pipe Network Design & Installation

New-build & retrofit

Full water-supply network design for new buildings and estate developments, from source and storage through to the last tap — sized, routed and pressure-tested before handover.
