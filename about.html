The team behind Abuja's pipework

Dream Flush Plumbing and Integrated Services is a licensed contracting outfit based in Jahi 1, Gwarinpa — built on the idea that plumbing work should be done once, done properly, and left tidy.

Our story

From single callouts to full estate contracts

Dream Flush started out fixing household leaks and blocked drains around Gwarinpa. As demand grew, so did the scope of the work — borehole projects, estate-wide drainage networks, and maintenance contracts for facilities that can't afford downtime.

Today the same principle still runs the business: turn up on time, quote honestly, use the right fitting for the job, and leave the site cleaner than we found it.

What we stand for

Three principles on every job

Diagnose before we touch a spanner

We trace the actual source of a fault instead of patching the symptom — fewer repeat callouts, lower long-term cost.

Fittings sized to spec, not guessed

Pipe diameters and pressure ratings are chosen for your building's actual demand, not whatever's on the van.

Straight answers, written quotes

You get a clear scope and price before work starts, and a plain explanation of what went wrong and why.
